About

My drive to pursue web development rooted from my passion for music. I run a YouTube channel where I post myself playing bass guitar. My viewers wanted a site where they could view the music written out which led me to create a simple Google Site. As my site became more popular I became frustrated with the limitations of the Google Site template. This led me to learn web development on my own and rebuilding my site from scratch was the perfect project.

I graduated UC Irvine in 2015 with a B.S. in Computer Science with a specialization in Information. Shortly after graduating I accepted a Technical Support Engineer role in Irvine. It was not a software developer position some of my roles included troubleshooting code and developing sample scripts for customers. While I did enjoy my time in Technical Support, I decided to leave to pursue a career in web development. Below you can find several of my web development projects.
